GMR Group is a comprehensive enterprise consisting of Pagani (China) Automobile Co., Ltd., Pagani UK Ltd., and GruppeM Racing Team. As an independent entity, we work closely with our partners to leverage synergies and achieve outstanding results. By combining global expertise and cultural diversity, we continuously drive the development of our brands.

We focus on the Greater China and Asia-Pacific markets, as evidenced by the establishment of Pagani (China) Automobile Co., Ltd. we have strategically positioned ourselves with showrooms and service centers in Shanghai, Hong Kong, and Taiwan, becoming one of the most respected supercar brands in the region.

Excellence in customer service is at the core of our values. We invest in service personnel, training systems, and state-of-the-art facilities, establishing a robust network to provide comprehensive aftersales support to owners. Our acclaimed aftersales department at the Pagani headquarters ranks first in the world, showcasing our commitment to excellence.

In addition to the realm of supercars, GMR Group proudly supports the GruppeM Racing Team. Since its establishment in the early 2000s, the team has earned the respect of manufacturers and competitors alike. In 2005, the GruppeM Racing Team made history in the International Automobile Federation GT Championship, achieving an unprecedented record of winning all 11 races, a feat yet to be surpassed.

Welcome to our world and experience the limitless excitement brought to you by GMR Group!
